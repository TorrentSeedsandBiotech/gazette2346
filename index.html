Gazette Notification No.: S.O. 2346(E)

Title of Biostimulant:

Mixture of Seaweed extract; Humic and Fulvic acid, Amino acids and Vitamins (Liquid)

Composition:

Sr. No. Ingredients Content (%)
1 Sargassum wightii extract per cent. by weight min 20%
2 Glycine per cent. by weight min 10%
3 Potassium fulvic humate per cent. by weight min (Source: Leonardite) 10%
4 Vitamin C per cent. by weight min 2.0%
5 Water per cent. by weight Q.S.
6 Total per cent. 100%

Recommended Crop: Cotton

Recommended Dose: Two foliar applications at 2 l/ha.
